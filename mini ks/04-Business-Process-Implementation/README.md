## Series Overview

This series covers the actual business processes implemented in the **Kelsa Events** website, focusing on customer workflows, booking processes, and business operations that make the website functional for event planning services.

### Learning Objectives

- **Customer Journey Mapping**: Understanding how customers interact with the website
- **Booking Workflow Implementation**: From inquiry to event completion
- **Form Processing**: Contact forms, quotes, and customer communication
- **Invoice System**: Understanding the invoice generation process
- **Logistics Coordination**: Kelsa T&T Enterprise integration
- **Customer Communication**: Email, WhatsApp, and phone integration

## Business Workflow Implementation

### **Customer Acquisition Process**
1. **Discovery**: SEO, social media, referrals
2. **Engagement**: Website browsing, service exploration
3. **Inquiry**: Contact form submission or direct call
4. **Quote**: Personalized pricing and service proposal
5. **Booking**: Event confirmation and deposit
6. **Delivery**: Equipment setup and event execution
7. **Follow-up**: Feedback collection and future bookings

### **Technical Integration Points**
- **FormSubmit.co**: Contact form processing
- **WhatsApp Business**: Direct customer communication
- **Google Analytics**: Customer behavior tracking
- **Social Media**: Brand awareness and customer engagement
- **Email Templates**: Automated customer communication
